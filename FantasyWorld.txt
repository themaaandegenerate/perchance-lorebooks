Adolion is a large fantasy world. It is comprised of a large number of races such as elves, demons, humans, dwarves, goblins, and more. It has medieval technology, magic, and monsters. 

Aegis City is the capital city of the Darran Kingdom. It is the largest city on the continent of Sebela. Its ruler is King Davolan. It is otherwise known as the Holy City. 

The Adventurer's Guild is an organization that manages the many adventurers in Adolion. They are a neutral party and manage the acquisition of quests, adventurer registration, and rewards. The Adventurer's Guild is necessary to keep the world safe for commoners. The Adventurer's Guild consists of two areas, the Guild Reception and the Tavern. 

The Holy Church is dedicated to the worship of Boher, the God of Life. The Holy Church is in a constant war against any necromancers or undead. Boher's symbol is a golden sun. Boher's followers seek out and destroy demons. 

Adventurers are ranked by the Adventurer's Guild to determine what kind of quests they can handle. An adventurer is ranked from the lowest rank of F to the highest rank of S, which is only achievable after completing 100 A-ranked quests. Special ranks can be awarded to adventurers who perform extraordinary feats to overcome calamitous entities or stop a city-wide threat.

The Raven Sisters, Morrigan and Cornelia Briarwood, were recently awarded with an X rank by the Adventurer's Guild for pacifying a crystal dragon that threatened to attack Aegis City and the surrounding lands.

The Delarise family is a noble family in Aegis City. First creators of Airships. Known for its weapon and magical item production. Strongest noble family in Aegis City.

Elves are a reclusive race who have only recently begun to integrate into human societies. Tall, lithe, beautiful, and able to live hundreds of years. 

Dwarves are stout folk, usually only about 4 feet tall. They tend to live in mountainous areas and are great crafters. Dwarves famously get along with most races and make the most loyal of friends. Dwarves obsess over their honor. 

Humans have the shortest lifespan of Adolions races. However, they are often ambitious and diverse, able to accomplish in a short amount of time what it takes other races centuries to complete. 

Demons are from the infernal planes. They vary in type. With some preferring to trick or deceive mortals into damnation, while others prefer to carve a bloody path of violence. To the mortal races, all demons are evil. 

Nagora Jungle - or the Vile Jungle - is a large jungle that covers 1/4th of Sebela. Extremely dangerous creatures wander there, mutated by the regions close proximity to the negative energy plane. 

Sebela is the largest continent on Adolion. It features a variety of biomes. It's most notable areas are: Aegis City, the Vile Jungle, the Mountains of Shadow, and Gragow. 

Quests are issued by the Adventurer's Guild after they receive a request from someone in need of help. Quests are easier the lower the rank and more difficult the higher the rank. Quests can take place anywhere in Sebela. Quest Location, Quest Target. 

Gragow was a large human city. Became a Lost Zone overnight. Eldritch creatures roam the streets and everyone that was inside is presumed dead. Gragow has some of the most advanced technology in Adolion lost within its walls. 

Lost Zones are areas in Adolion that the Adventurer's Guild has decided are too dangerous to investigate. Only foolhardy adventurers and treasure hunters dare try to brave these areas.

A Starfall is a rare celestial event that occurs when divine mana breaches the mortal realm. If a powerful mage is struck by this force survives the process of absorbing its transcendent energy, they will become Star-Touched. Just witnessing a Starfall alone is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, so someone becoming Star-Touched is treated as an impossibility.

If someone becomes Star-Touched, they will have extraordinarily high levels of mana, enhanced physical abilities, gain the capacity to cast divine magic (though there are no known divine spells for a Star-Touched to learn). The only reason Ceres Vaelis, the first Star-Touched in recorded history, is alive is because her parents sacrificed themselves to reforge her body with the divine magic.

Dion Vaelis is the deceased father of Ceres Vaelis. He was an muscular, average height human with short brown hair, golden eyes, and a strong personal sense of justice. He was a mercenary who mainly earned his keep by helping villages deal with nearby bandits and monsters. His skill with a sword was more suited for a blademaster than a simple mercenary. They eventually married and she took his last name, becoming Alina Vaelis.

Alina reforged Ceres' body with the divine mana while Dion shielded them from the mana storm long enough for the process to succeed.
